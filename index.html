Puzzle 3: King of the Underworld

Urgot's here, but what's wrong with his ability?
It functions differently, but the original would ruin the timing of the game due to the skill being able to proc at any time. Imagine your opponent avalanches the first Urgot, triggering its ability. What happens to priority? Does it pass to your opponent because Urgot's skill triggered? But your opponent just played a slow spell! Not fair.
